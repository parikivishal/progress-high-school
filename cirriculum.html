Curriculum Overview

At Progress High School, we follow a comprehensive curriculum designed to nurture the intellectual, physical, social, and emotional development of our students. Our curriculum is aligned with national educational standards while incorporating innovative teaching methodologies.

We believe in providing a balanced education that prepares students not just for examinations but for life beyond school. Our approach emphasizes:

  • Conceptual understanding over rote learning
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Creativity and innovation
  • Communication and collaboration
  • Character development and values education

Our Curriculum Framework

A structured approach to comprehensive education

Core Academic Subjects

Our curriculum includes rigorous academic subjects that form the foundation of knowledge and skills:

  • Language Arts (English and Telugu)
  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Studies (History, Geography, Civics)
  • Computer Science
  • Hindi/Sanskrit (Second Language)

Co-Curricular Activities

We complement academic learning with activities that develop creativity and physical fitness:

  • Visual Arts (Drawing, Painting, Crafts)
  • Performing Arts (Music, Dance, Drama)
  • Physical Education and Sports
  • Yoga and Meditation
  • Environmental Education
  • Life Skills and Value Education

Enrichment Programs

We offer specialized programs to enhance learning and prepare students for future challenges:

  • STEM Education (Atal Tinkering Lab)
  • Entrepreneurship and Financial Literacy
  • Public Speaking and Debate
  • Competitive Exam Preparation
  • Career Guidance and Counseling
  • Community Service and Leadership

Grade-wise Curriculum

Tailored learning experiences for each developmental stage

Pre-Primary (Nursery to PP2)

Our pre-primary curriculum is based on the Chrysalis International framework, focusing on holistic development through play-based learning.

Key Focus Areas:
  • Language and Communication
  • Number Concepts
  • Environmental Awareness
  • Fine and Gross Motor Skills
  • Creative Expression
  • Social Skills
  • Emotional Development
  • Basic Life Skills

Our approach emphasizes learning through exploration, hands-on activities, stories, rhymes, games, and art. We create a nurturing environment where children develop curiosity, confidence, and a love for learning.

Primary (Grades 1-5)

Our primary curriculum continues with the Chrysalis International framework, transitioning students from play-based to more structured learning while maintaining engagement and creativity.

Core Subjects:
  • English Language Arts
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental Science
  • Social Studies
  • Telugu/Hindi
  • Computer Science
  • Art and Craft
  • Physical Education

Our teaching methodology emphasizes conceptual understanding, critical thinking, and application of knowledge. We incorporate project-based learning, group activities, and educational technology to make learning engaging and effective.

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Our middle school curriculum follows the State Board syllabus with enrichment through our integrated IIT-NEET Foundation Program, preparing students for higher academic challenges.

Core Subjects:
  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Studies
  • Telugu/Hindi
  • Computer Science
  • Physical Education
  • Art Education

Our approach emphasizes deeper conceptual understanding, analytical thinking, and problem-solving skills. Students engage in laboratory experiments, research projects, debates, and presentations to develop comprehensive subject knowledge and practical skills.

High School (Grades 9-10)

Our high school curriculum follows the State Board syllabus with intensive preparation for board examinations and competitive exams like IIT-JEE, NEET, and Olympiads.

Core Subjects:
  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Studies
  • Telugu/Hindi
  • Computer Applications
  • Physical Education
  • Career Guidance

Our teaching approach combines rigorous academic preparation with practical applications and real-world connections. Students receive specialized coaching, regular assessments, and personalized feedback to achieve excellence in board examinations and competitive exams.

Our Teaching Methodology

Innovative approaches to effective learning

Student-Centered Learning

We place students at the center of the learning process, encouraging active participation, inquiry, and self-directed learning.

Experiential Learning

We believe in learning by doing, incorporating hands-on activities, experiments, field trips, and projects into our curriculum.

Technology Integration

We integrate digital tools and resources to enhance learning, develop digital literacy, and prepare students for a technology-driven world.

Personalized Approach

We recognize that each student is unique, with different learning styles, strengths, and areas for growth, and tailor our teaching accordingly.

Assessment System

Our assessment system is designed to be comprehensive, continuous, and constructive, focusing on both formative and summative evaluation to track student progress and provide meaningful feedback.

Types of Assessments:
  • Formative Assessments: Regular quizzes, class participation, homework, projects, and activities to monitor ongoing progress
  • Summative Assessments: Unit tests, term examinations, and annual examinations to evaluate overall understanding
  • Performance Assessments: Projects, presentations, lab work, and portfolios to evaluate application of knowledge and skills
  • Behavioral Assessments: Evaluation of social skills, values, attitudes, and character development

We provide detailed progress reports to parents every term, along with regular parent-teacher meetings to discuss student progress and areas for improvement. Our goal is to use assessment not just for evaluation but as a tool for enhancing learning.

Special Educational Programs

Enhancing learning through specialized initiatives

Atal Tinkering Lab

Our state-of-the-art Atal Tinkering Lab provides students with hands-on experience in STEM education, fostering innovation, creativity, and problem-solving skills through projects involving robotics, electronics, 3D printing, and more.

IIT-NEET Foundation Program

Our integrated foundation program for grades 6-10 builds strong conceptual understanding and problem-solving skills in Mathematics and Science, preparing students for future success in competitive exams like IIT-JEE and NEET.

Olympiad Training

We provide specialized training for various national and international Olympiads in Mathematics, Science, English, and Computer Science, challenging students to excel beyond the regular curriculum and compete at higher levels.
