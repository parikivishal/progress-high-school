Welcome to Progress High School

Progress begins with curiosity - tinker, innovate and shape the future!

Welcome to Progress High School! Since our establishment in 1993, we have remained committed to providing quality education that nurtures both intellect and character. Located in Reddy Colony, Hanamkonda, our school has been appeared with the vision of making education accessible and meaningful for children from all backgrounds. With a team of 50 dedicated and passionate teachers, we focus on fostering academic excellence, creativity, and essential life skills that prepare our students for the future.

At Progress High School, we believe that education goes beyond textbooks. Our approach emphasizes holistic development encouraging students to grow through academics, leadership opportunities, and extracurricular activities. With over 31 years of experience, we continue to uphold our values of discipline, innovation, and integrity, shaping responsible citizens who are ready to face the world with confidence. We warmly invite you to be part of our journey toward excellence.

Our Vision

To empower students with knowledge, character, and real-world skills, creating responsible, confident, and compassionate individuals prepared to face the challenges of the future.

Our Aim

Our aim is to elicit knowledge, nurture curiosity, and bring out the hidden potential in every child through meaningful, engaging, and holistic learning experiences.

Our Mission

To provide quality and affordable education to children from all backgrounds, nurturing academic excellence, strong values, and holistic development through a dedicated team of educators.
