Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our school

Our school hours are from 8:30 AM to 4:00 PM Monday through Friday. The school office is open from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM on weekdays and from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM on Saturdays.

You can apply for admission by visiting our Admissions page and filling out the online application form. Alternatively, you can visit our school office to collect a physical application form. For more details, please visit our Admission Process page.

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (cricket, basketball, volleyball, athletics), arts (music, dance, drama, painting), clubs (science, math, literary, eco), and more. These activities are designed to help students develop their talents and interests beyond academics.

Yes, we provide transportation services covering major areas of the city. Our buses are equipped with GPS tracking and are maintained regularly to ensure safety. Transportation fees vary based on the distance from school. Please contact our office for specific route information and fees.

Parents can get involved through our Parent-Teacher Association (PTA), volunteering for school events, attending parent-teacher meetings, and participating in school development committees. We believe in strong parent-school partnerships and welcome parent involvement in various aspects of school life.
